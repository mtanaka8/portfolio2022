Qatar, Doha Tourism Logo

Creating a Logo for a City

We were tasked to create a logo for a city of our choice. The city I've chosen was Doha which is located in Qatar. I became drawn to the city's attractions and its significant culture.

Doha is a beautiful city with many attractions which gave me a lot of ideas to start with. I had to undergo the sketching process a few times but I have found three concepts to work with. Eventually, one of the three concepts has been chosen. I experimented with different colors and my final logo for Doha has been made.

Client: Emily Moody
Service: Visual Identity Design, Graphic Design
Skills: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop
